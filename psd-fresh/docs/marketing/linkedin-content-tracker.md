## 📝 Post Templates Library

### Contrarian Post Template
```
I [audited/analyzed] [X] [industry] [things] in [location] last week.

[Number] of them had the same [Y] problems:

❌ [Problem 1]
❌ [Problem 2]
❌ [Problem 3]

Here's what the [number] [thing] doing it right had:

✅ [Solution 1]
✅ [Solution 2]
✅ [Solution 3]

The result: [specific outcome with number]

[Contrarian insight]

[Engagement question]?
```

### Data/Stats Post Template
```
[Thing] improved [metric] by [X]% for one of my [client type] clients.

The numbers:
• Before: [stat]
• After: [stat]
• Impact: [specific result with number]

Why? [Explanation with insight]

[Industry context/statistic]

[If/then statement with pain point]

[Engagement question]?
```

### Engagement/Poll Post Template
```
Quick poll for [target audience]:

[Question with options A/B/C/D/E]

I ask because [insight/statistic about problem]

[The consequence of not solving it]

Your [thing] needs a [outcome]. What's yours?

👇 Drop your answer below
```

### Case Study Post Template
```
[Timeframe] ago, [client type] came to me with [problem].

They were [specific pain point with emotion].

Here's what we did:

1️⃣ [Action 1]
2️⃣ [Action 2]
3️⃣ [Action 3]

The result: [specific outcome with numbers]

[Emotional payoff quote or reflection]

[Connection to broader lesson/philosophy]

[Engagement question]?
```

## 🔥 Engagement Rules (Non-Negotiable)

### Golden Hour (First 60 minutes after posting)
- [ ] Reply to EVERY comment within 5 minutes
- [ ] Like all comments
- [ ] Ask follow-up questions on comments
- [ ] Comment on 5 other posts immediately

### Daily Engagement (30 min routine)
- [ ] Check "Who viewed your profile" → Message 3-5 warm leads
- [ ] Send 5-10 connection requests
- [ ] Comment on 5 posts from target audience
- [ ] Reply to all DMs within 2 hours

### Weekly Engagement
- [ ] Sunday: Review metrics, plan next week
- [ ] Monday: Post + heavy engagement
- [ ] Wednesday: Mid-week check-in
- [ ] Friday: Week recap post
