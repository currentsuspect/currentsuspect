## 🔧 RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

### Step 1: Find Decision Makers

**For Clinics:**
1. Search: "[Clinic name] managing director" or "[Clinic name] medical director"
2. Check LinkedIn for hospital staff
3. Look for "About Us" or "Management Team" page on website
4. Common titles: Medical Director, Managing Director, Administrator, CEO

**For Law Firms:**
1. Search: "[Firm name] managing partner" or "[Firm name] senior partner"
2. Check "Our Team" or "Partners" page
3. LinkedIn search firm name + "partner"
4. Common titles: Managing Partner, Senior Partner, Founder, Director

**For Schools:**
1. Search: "[School name] principal" or "[School name] head teacher"
2. Check "About" or "Staff" page
3. LinkedIn search school name + "principal"
4. Common titles: Principal, Head Teacher, Headmaster, Director

### Step 2: Find Email Addresses

**Method 1: Website Contact Page**
- Check /contact or /about pages
- Look for "info@" or "admin@" emails
- Sometimes decision maker emails are listed directly

**Method 2: Hunter.io (Free)**
- Go to hunter.io
- Enter company domain (e.g., karenhospital.org)
- Shows pattern: firstname@company.com or first.last@company.com
- Try variations based on decision maker name

**Method 3: LinkedIn**
- View their profile
- Check "Contact info" section
- Some list emails publicly

**Method 4: Guess & Verify**
- Common patterns:
  - firstname@company.co.ke
  - firstname.lastname@company.co.ke
  - f.lastname@company.co.ke
  - firstname@company.com
- Verify with: verify-email.org or mailtester.com

**Method 5: WHOIS Lookup**
- Go to whois.com
- Enter domain name
- Sometimes shows admin email

### Step 3: Website Audit (Find Pain Points)

**Quick 5-Minute Audit Checklist:**

☐ **Mobile Experience**
- Open site on phone
- Does it load in <3 seconds?
- Is navigation easy?
- Are CTAs visible without scrolling?

☐ **Booking/Contact Process**
- How many clicks to book/call/email?
- Is there WhatsApp integration?
- How many form fields required?
- Is there online scheduling?

☐ **Trust Signals**
- Are there testimonials?
- Is there a team/about page with photos?
- Are credentials displayed?
- Any awards/recognition shown?

☐ **SEO Basics**
- Does the title tag match what they do?
- Is there a clear H1 headline?
- Do pages have meta descriptions?

☐ **Conversion Elements**
- Is there a clear call-to-action?
- Is contact info easy to find?
- Are there multiple contact options?

## 🎣 SPECIFIC OBSERVATIONS TO USE

### Common Clinic Website Issues:
- "No online booking — requires phone call during business hours only"
- "Contact form has 8+ fields — high abandonment"
- "Mobile site loads in 6+ seconds — losing mobile patients"

- "Services page is just a list — no patient journey guidance"

- "No case evaluation form — missing qualified leads"

- "Practice areas buried in navigation — hard to find"

- "Inquiry form sends to general email — no tracking"
- "No virtual tour or photos of facilities"
- "Calendar/events not updated — looks abandoned"

- "Contact info hidden on subpages"
